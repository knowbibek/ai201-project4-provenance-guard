## Problem & Goals

Creative platforms (writing, music, art) need a way to tell readers whether a submitted piece
was likely written by a human or generated by AI — **to protect attribution and give context, not
to police creativity.** Provenance Guard is a backend any such platform can plug into. It:

1. Classifies submitted text (human / AI / uncertain),
2. Scores confidence honestly instead of forcing a binary,
3. Surfaces a plain-language transparency label, and
4. Lets creators appeal a classification.

Design bias: **a false positive (calling a human's work AI) is worse than a false negative.**
Confidence thresholds and label wording should reflect that asymmetry.
